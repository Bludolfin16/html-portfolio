The Best Anime According to Blu

My top 3 anime for now.

One Piece

This is one of my favorite anime. A person can grow up with long running anime like this.

Demon Slayer

This is an isekai anime, where the MC dies and gets a new life in another world. I think this is one of the cuter ones out there.
